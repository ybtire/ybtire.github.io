-Fuel Induction System

When did you last service your automobile's fuel and air induction system?

Have you experienced:

Hard start, rough idle and/or hesitation?
Decrease in milage?
Loss of power?

These are common drivability problems associated with deposits in the fuel and air induction system.

Our MotorVac system connects to your car's engine and circulates a mixture of MotorVac solution and fuel through all fuel system components. This cleaning solution removes carbon, gum and varnish from your engine, from intake to exaust. After the service your engine and fuel system will function more efficiently.

MotorVac Carbon Clean Fuel System Service:

Restores "new car" performance.
Restores fuel economy
Helps the environment by lowering car's emissions

When was the last time you had your automobile's transmission serviced?

13 million transmissions fail every year
